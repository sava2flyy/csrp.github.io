Moderation — fair, transparent, and appealable

Our moderation philosophy prioritizes fair treatment, evidence-based decisions, and a clear route to appeal. This keeps roleplay fun and the community safe.

1) Report & Evidence

Players report incidents via our Discord forms or in-game report tool. Staff collect screenshots, logs, and server evidence before action.

2) Investigation & Notice

An impartial investigator reviews evidence; if action is needed we issue warnings or temporary sanctions with a clear reason message and duration.

3) Appeal Process

Appeals are handled on Discord under a dedicated appeals channel. You can submit evidence and request review. Appeals are adjudicated by a separate appeals team.

4) Transparency

Final decisions include a short explanation. Repeat offenders follow an escalated policy. Community moderators publish anonymized case statistics monthly.
