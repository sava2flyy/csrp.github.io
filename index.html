About Us

We are a community-driven ER:LC California server inspired by immersive policing and emergency response roleplay. Our goal is to provide realistic, scripted-free, and fun roleplay with active staff, custom maps, and integrated dispatch systems. Whether you’re a trainee or a veteran, jump in and help keep our city safe.

Our server emphasizes teamwork, in-character professionalism, and ongoing support for members. We run regular events, training sessions, and have a responsive moderation team.
